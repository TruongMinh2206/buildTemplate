Frequently Asked Questions
Answers & Questions
From simple queries to more detailed concerns, we've gathered the most frequently asked questions to help you find the answers you need.
What countries do you ship to?
We currently ship to the United States, Canada, Australia and the UK. To enquire about shipping to a different destination, please contact us.
How long will it take to receive my order?
Standard shipping normally takes 35 days. Next day shipping is available on all domestic orders (for an additional charge). International shipping times depend on the products and destination (estimated at checkout).
Can I return or exchange a product if I'm not satisfied?
Yes. We have a 30-day return policy for items in the original packaging. If you've opened the product but found it defective or not to your satisfaction, you can bring it back within 14 days for an exchange.
Do you provide technical support or assistance for setting up purchased electronics?
Yes, we have a dedicated technical support team available both online and in-store. They can assist you with setup, troubleshooting, and any technical questions you might have about your purchase.
